But Why Star Wars?

Star Wars actually holds a special place in my heart and my entire family. My grand-mother and step grand-father had their first date watching the premiers of episode 6, A New Hope in 1977. I've read many of the Star Wars books and obviously have seen all the movies. It's my favorite IP!

Movies

Episode I: The Phantom Menace

Set thirty-two years before the original trilogy, "The Phantom Menace" introduces a young Anakin Skywalker, a boy with a unique connection to the Force. He becomes embroiled in the galactic turmoil along with Obi-Wan Kenobi and his master Qui-Gon Jinn during the rise of the Sith's dark plans.

Episode II: Attack of the Clones

Ten years after the Phantom Menace, the galaxy is on the brink of civil war, orchestrated by a secret separatist group. As Obi-Wan uncovers this conspiracy, Anakin Skywalker, now an apprentice, struggles with his emotions, a foreboding threat to his Jedi principles.

Episode III: Revenge of the Sith

The Clone Wars culminate in a dark victory for the Sith. Democracy falls as Palpatine declares himself Emperor. Betrayed by his friend Anakin, who has turned to the dark side and become Darth Vader, Obi-Wan must make a fateful choice that changes the galaxy's destiny.

Episode IV: A New Hope

With the galaxy under the oppressive hold of the Galactic Empire, farm boy Luke Skywalker discovers his destiny when he obtains a message from the captured Princess Leia. Alongside Han Solo, Obi-Wan Kenobi, and others, Luke embarks on a daring mission to rescue the princess and rebel against the evil Empire.

Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back

As the Rebels suffer a devastating attack from the Empire, Luke trains with Yoda to become a Jedi. Dark secrets come to light as the Empire hunts the Rebel forces, culminating in a dire confrontation between Luke and the malevolent Darth Vader.

Episode VI: Return of the Jedi

In a final battle for the galaxy's freedom, Luke and his friends launch a mission to rescue Han Solo from the vile Jabba the Hutt and face the Empire in a decisive confrontation. As the Rebel Alliance challenges the second Death Star, Luke confronts Darth Vader and the truth of his lineage.

Episode VII: The Force Awakens

Set thirty years after the defeat of the Galactic Empire, "The Force Awakens" follows Rey, a scavenger whose life changes when she encounters Finn, a defected stormtrooper. They join forces with Han Solo to confront the sinister First Order, a new threat led by Kylo Ren, and find the missing Luke Skywalker.

Episode VIII: The Last Jedi

"The Last Jedi" depicts the escalating conflict between the First Order and the Resistance. As Rey seeks to learn the ways of the Force under Luke Skywalker's reluctant tutelage, the Resistance, led by General Leia Organa, attempts to fend off the First Order, whose ferocity has been underestimated.

Episode IX: The Rise of Skywalker

The final episode of the Skywalker saga, "The Rise of Skywalker," confronts new legends and old enemies. Rey continues her Jedi training and must face her heritage, while the surviving Resistance members make a final stand against Kylo Ren and the First Order, revealing unexpected ties to the dark legacy of the Sith.

Characters

Luke Skywalker

Luke Skywalker, a Force-sensitive human male, was a legendary Jedi Master who fought in the Galactic Civil War during the reign of the Galactic Empire. Along with his companions, Princess Leia Organa and Captain Han Solo, Skywalker served on the side of the Alliance to Restore the Republic.

Leia Organa

Leia Organa, a Force-sensitive human female, was one of the greatest leaders of the Rebel Alliance, fighting against the tyranny of the Galactic Empire.

Han Solo

Han Solo, a human male, was a smuggler from the manufacturing planet Corellia who achieved galactic fame as a member of the Rebel Alliance and later the New Republic.

Planets

Hoth

Hoth is the sixth planet in the remote system of the same name, and was the site of the Rebel Alliance's Echo Base. It is a world of snow and ice, surrounded by numerous moons, and home to deadly creatures like the wampa.

Coruscant

Coruscant, also known as the Imperial Center during the Imperial Era, is the political hub of the galaxy. Towering skyscrapers and futuristic technology make it a significant and influential planet.

Tatooine

Tatooine is a desert planet in a binary star system, and the first of three planets orbiting the suns. It is characterized by its arid climate and iconic sand dunes. It is the homeworld of Anakin and Luke Skywalker, who would go on to shape galactic history.

Starships

Millennium Falcon

The Millennium Falcon is a YT-1300 light freighter of the YT-1300f variety. Its aged appearance belies numerous advanced modifications to boost the ship's speed, weaponry, and shield, including a hyperdrive capable of traveling through hyperspace.

X-Wing Starfighter

The X-Wing starfighter is a versatile Rebel Alliance craft that balances speed with firepower, equipped with four laser cannons and two proton torpedo launchers, capable of going head-to-head with the Empire's best fighters.

Imperial Star Destroyer

The Imperial-class Star Destroyer, a symbol of the Empire's military might, has greater firepower than a full fleet of ordinary battleships. It can engage multiple enemy craft simultaneously, deploying TIE fighters for both attack and defense.
